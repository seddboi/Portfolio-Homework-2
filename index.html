My Initial Beginnings

I am a 22 year old Applied Mathematics Major, who just recently graduated from the University of California Riverside in the Summer of 2020. A majority of my college career, I spent most of my time studying, participating in research, and growing my Mathematics knowledge by being an avid member of the UCR Mathematics Club. Apart from my endeavors in school, I found small interests in studying Python coding and gaining Google certificates for Ad Search. These small interests gradually increased as I was ending my college career.

What caused my change in Career Path?

Web Development became the newest venture of mine, since it is a growing industry and I have a shared interest in learning the "behind the scenes" knowledge that allows computers to function. Initially, I started off with very little knowledge of developing coherent code. This, along with my rapidly growing interests in Web Development and Programming, urged me to sign up for the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp offered at the UCR Extension.

What have I gained from this?

As of today I am religiously practicing HTML, CSS Styling, and Javascript (in terms of Web development). I also am proficient in Python, as I have gained a Code Academy certificate through about 6+ months worth of Python practice and study within their program. Both programs also taught me how to establish a profile among Github, from which I learned to understand the Git version control system. Now I use all of this knowledge to establish a stronger foundation in my journey in becoming a Full Full-Stack Web Developer/Programmer.
